Joel Castillo-Rey

PhD researcher at BCAM

Since September 2022, I have been conducting my research at the Basque Center for Applied Mathematics (BCAM) in Bilbao under the supervision of Ilya Smirnov in the Singularity Theory and Algebraic Geometry group. My interests lie between Commutative Algebra and Algebraic Geometry, and my research has been mainly focused in the study of singularities in positive characteristic.
